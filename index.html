Common questions

Good to know

How much does a home survey cost?

It depends on the property's size, type and survey level. Level 2 surveys start from £420, Level 3 from £650 — use the instant quote tool for a price tailored to your property.

Level 2 or Level 3 — which do I need?

Level 2 suits conventional, modern homes in reasonable condition. Level 3 is more detailed and best for older, larger, listed or altered properties. Not sure? We'll point you to the right one, free.

How quickly can you survey?

We typically attend within a few days of booking and deliver your full report within 5–7 days of the inspection.

Are your surveyors RICS qualified?

Yes — 100%. Every survey is signed off by an RICS-qualified surveyor, and we're a RICS-regulated firm.
