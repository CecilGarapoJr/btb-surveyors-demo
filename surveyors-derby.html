Local knowledge

Surveyors who know Derby homes

Derby's housing is wonderfully varied — and every era has its quirks. Our local RICS surveyors know exactly what to check, street by street.

  • Victorian & Edwardian terraces in the city, Normanton and near the old railway and Rolls-Royce works — rising damp, roof spread and tired bay windows.
  • Grand Victorian villas & the Darley Abbey conservation area — this historic mill village brings period detail and listed-building considerations.
  • Flood risk near the River Derwent — we factor in flood-zone and ground/brownfield considerations on riverside and former industrial plots.
  • New-build estates in Chellaston, Oakwood & Mickleover — snagging and developer defects before you complete.
