Common questions

Valuation questions, answered

What's the difference between a valuation and a survey?

A valuation gives a single, formal figure of what a property is worth. A survey inspects the building's condition and reports defects. Need a value for a transaction, tax or legal purpose? You need a valuation.

How long does a RICS valuation take?

We usually attend within a few days of booking, with your written valuation following two to three working days after the inspection.

Will it be accepted by HMRC, my lender or the housing association?

Yes. Every report is signed by an RICS-qualified valuer and written in the format the relevant body expects — which is exactly why a free agent appraisal won't do.
